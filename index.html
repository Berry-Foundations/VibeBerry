Commands

Prefixes: vibe vibeberry vb music

<prefix> help: Shows the help menu.
<prefix> connect: Connects to the voice channel command user is in.
<prefix> disconnect: Disconnects from the voice channel it is in.
<prefix> play <music>: Searches for <music> and plays it.
<prefix> pause: Pauses the music it is playing, if any.
<prefix> resume: Resumes the paused music, if any music paused.
<prefix> stop: Stops the music, if any.
